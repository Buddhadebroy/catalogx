Transform your store with WooCommerce Catalog Mode & enquiry System - Create professional catalogs with quote requests and wholesale pricing - powered by WordPress

= Detailed Feature Breakdown =

==✌️Catalog ==
Control store pricing visibility with ease. This tool creates exclusivity, protects sensitive pricing info, and encourages direct customer engagement for more personalized sales and higher conversions.

= Key Features: =
🛍️ **Enable Catalog Mode Across the Site** : Showcase your products by enabling catalog mode for the entire site or specific products, allowing customers to browse without the option to purchase.
🔒 **Create a Private Store Experience**: Restrict access by hiding prices and the add-to-cart button until users log in, creating an exclusive, private shopping experience for select customers.
📈 **Strategically Manage Price Visibility**: Boost lead generation by controlling when and where product prices are visible, encouraging users to engage and inquire about your offerings.

==✌️ Enquiry ==
Place enquiry buttons across the store to capture leads, handle bulk orders or high-value purchases, gauge demand, and boost conversions and customer satisfaction.

= Key Features: =
📍 **Place Enquiry & Quote Request Buttons Anywhere** : Maximize visibility by placing enquiry and quote request buttons anywhere on your site using shortcodes.
🔄 **Allow Inquiries for 'Out of Stock' Items** : Keep customers engaged by letting them inquire about out-of-stock products, ensuring you don’t miss potential sales.
🔗 **Seamlessly Integrate enquiry Button with Page Builders** : Add an enquiry button to pages built with your preferred page builder for a smooth, cohesive shopping experience.
🔍 **Track Customer Inquiries for Product Insights** : Gain valuable insights into product demand and customer preferences by tracking all incoming inquiries.
🛒 **Enable Bulk or High-Value Order Inquiries [PRO]** : Let customers inquire about bulk or high-value orders using the Enquiry Cart, designed for large or custom purchases.
✏️ **Customize enquiry Forms to Match Your Brand [PRO]** : Craft enquiry forms that align with your brand’s aesthetic and gather the most relevant customer information.
📊 **Monitor & Manage Inquiries via the Enquiry Cart [PRO]** : Easily track and manage all inquiries through the Enquiry Cart, making it simple to follow up and convert leads.
🎨 **Choose from 5 Advanced Enquiry Form Templates [PRO]** : Select from five customizable enquiry form templates to suit your business needs and brand aesthetic.


==✌️ Quotation ==
Enable customers to request personalized quotes directly from the product catalog, streamlining custom orders and boosting sales, especially for B2B, wholesale, and custom businesses.

= Key Features: =
➕ **Simplify Quote Requests with the “Add to Quote” Button** : Make it easy for customers to request quotes by displaying the "Add to Quote" button on product pages.
🛒 **Offer Quote Requests for All or Selected Products** : Allow visitors to request quotes for either all or specific products, catering to diverse customer requirements.
🔐 **Restrict Quote Requests to Registered Users** : Limit quote requests to registered users, ensuring you receive qualified leads and engage serious buyers.
🔍 **Quickly Find Customer Quotations [PRO]** : Easily locate specific customer quotations using the Quick Search feature for efficient quote management.
🤝 **Ensure Seamless MultiVendor Plugin Compatibility [PRO]** : Enjoy a smooth experience by ensuring compatibility with MultiVendor plugins like MultiVendorX.
📧 **Customize Emails and Quotations to Match Your Brand [PRO]** : Reflect your brand’s tone and style by personalizing your email communications and quotations.

==✌️ Wholesale ==
Effortlessly manage retail and wholesale operations. Create separate pricing, segment customers, and offer exclusive wholesale rates while protecting retail prices and controlling coupon usage.

= Key Features: =
🛑 **Hide Retail Prices from Wholesale Customers** : Focus on wholesale pricing strategies by hiding retail prices from wholesale customers to maintain pricing structure integrity.
🚫 **Prevent WooCommerce Coupons for Wholesale Customers** : Protect your profit margins by blocking the use of WooCommerce coupons by wholesale customers, ensuring your pricing remains intact.
💲**Display Both Retail and Wholesale Prices to Business Users** : Offer transparency and flexibility by showing both retail and wholesale prices to business customers, allowing them to make informed decisions.
💸 **Configure Fixed or Percentage Discounts for Customer Segments** : Tailor your pricing strategy by offering fixed amount or percentage-based discounts that suit different customer groups or purchase volumes.
🏷️ **Set Up Custom Prices for Each User or Product [PRO]** : Create personalized pricing for each customer or product, providing unique offers that meet specific needs or business requirements.

==✌️ Dynamic Rule-Based Pricing ==
Boost sales with targeted pricing. Create custom discounts based on user roles, product categories, and more to drive conversions, loyalty, and revenue.

= Key Features: =
🎯 **Create Custom Discount Rules** : Offer exclusive deals to specific user roles like subscribers or wholesale buyers with custom discount rules.
🏷️ **Target Promotions by Products, Categories, or User Roles** : Maximize sales potential by targeting promotions based on products, categories, or user roles.
📊 **Add Multiple User Role Levels with Pricing Rules** : Enhance flexibility by adding user role levels, each with corresponding pricing rules.
🛒 **Encourage Larger Purchases with Tiered Discounts** : Offer tiered discounts based on product quantity or cart value to motivate larger purchases.
🔄 **Apply Discounts to Individual Products or Categories** : Apply discounts selectively to individual products or entire categories, depending on your strategy.
📋 **Prioritize Pricing Rules Easily** : Use a drag-and-drop interface to prioritize pricing rules and ensure the most effective discounts are applied first.

== Installation ==
Upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/catalogx directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
Use the Settings->CatalogX screen to configure the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Is CatalogX compatible with my theme? =
CatalogX is designed to work with most WooCommerce-compatible themes. However, we recommend testing it with your specific theme to ensure optimal compatibility.
= Can I use multiple features simultaneously? =
Yes, all features in CatalogX are designed to work together seamlessly. You can enable and customize each feature according to your needs.
= Is there a limit to the number of pricing rules I can create? =
There is no set limit to the number of pricing rules you can create with the Dynamic Rule-Based Pricing feature. However, we recommend keeping your rules manageable for optimal performance.
